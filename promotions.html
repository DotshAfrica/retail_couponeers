Manufacturer coupons

Manufacturer coupons are issued by the manufacturer or supplier of a product in order to increase sales and product awareness. Once accepted, the manufacturer owes the retailer the total discount amount accrued at the end of the agreed on period.

Because of this, manufacturer discounts have no negative effect on the profit margin of the retailer as the discounted difference is absorbed by the supplier

Return Coupons

A return coupon is issued to the customer by the retailer to increase customer loyalty. These promotions are driven by the retail store to increase the sales or to create awareness of the in-house products and brands.

In the case for Tuskys for example, return coupons would work perfectly for your Tuskys line of products to promote the brand and increase sale of the products.
However, unlike manufacturer coupons, kindly note that the cost of running return coupons is borne by the retailer in terms of the cost of the discounts issued.
