Why choose us

Retail Couponeers is a Kenyan based marketing company whose core business is to drive coupon based promotions on behalf of manufacturers and suppliers to the final consumer, through retailers such as Tuskys Supermarket.

We specialize in the generation, printing, branding, marketing and distribution of coupons throughout your retail network by working hand in hand with your suppliers.

Best Ever Solution

We are offering a system that will ensure our coupons drive your sales up while you focus on your core business of retailing. This we will achieve by:

Responsibility

Assuming the responsibility of seeing to the production of the coupons in all the stages involved, from generating, to editing and to printing.

Logistics

Taking up all the responsibilities involved in the logistics and management of the coupons once they are generated, till they are accessible to each and every one of your customers in all your branches.

Verification

Verifying and consolidating branch by branch sales reports that are involved with coupons, to ensure that there is no discrepancy in the coupons redeemed by your cashiers.

Liaison

Liaising with your existing manufacturers and suppliers to drive these campaigns on your behalf while you focus on the most important things in life.

Synchronization

Working closely and openly with your IT and operations departments to ensure synchrony of systems and operations and to promote a working culture of openness and teamwork.

Supplier Satisfaction

Taking responsibility for supplier account consolidation with regards to coupon sales and ensuring that those suppliers are issued with the appropriate credit notes which will enable you to recover the discounted amounts they owe you.
